The Honest Version

DocuFire is built natively on Apple's tools — SwiftUI and iCloud — which is why it works the way it does on iPad and Mac. A Windows or web version would mean rebuilding the app essentially from scratch on a different foundation. That's a real undertaking for a one-person team, not a quick add-on.

I'm not ruling it out. But before sinking months into a second version of the app, I want to know it's worth it to enough investigators to be worth the time away from improving what already exists. This page exists to find that out honestly.

If you sign up, I'll actually email you — whether the answer ends up being yes or not yet.
