Privacy Policy

DocuFire LLC · Effective June 1, 2026 · Last updated June 1, 2026
01

The Short Version

DocuFire does not collect, store, or transmit your case data. Everything you document — addresses, owner information, witness statements, photos, breaker panels — stays on your device. We never see it.

The only data that leaves your device is anonymous, non-identifiable diagnostic information used to keep the app stable. No names. No case details. No personal information of any kind.

02

Information We Do Not Collect

DocuFire is a local-first application. The following data types are entered by investigators and stored exclusively on-device:

  • Incident addresses and case numbers
  • Owner, occupant, and witness information
  • Insurance carrier and claim details
  • Scene photographs and diagram annotations
  • Breaker panel documentation
  • Origin and cause findings
  • Evidence logs and field notes
  • Exported PDF reports

03

Anonymous Diagnostics

DocuFire uses TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous, non-identifiable diagnostic data. This helps us identify crashes and improve app stability.

TelemetryDeck is specifically designed for privacy-conscious developers. It does not use cookies, does not track users across apps or websites, and cannot be used to identify any individual. Data collected includes:

  • Crash reports and error logs
  • App launch time and general performance metrics
  • iOS/iPadOS version and device category (not device ID)

This data is never linked to your identity, your cases, or any personal information. For more information, visit telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

04

AI Features & PII Protection

DocuFire includes an optional AI assistant feature. This feature is disabled by default and is never activated without explicit action by you.

If you choose to use the AI feature, it operates using your own API credentials connected directly to your chosen AI provider. DocuFire does not proxy, store, log, or have access to any content sent to or received from AI services.

PII is automatically excluded from all AI requests. DocuFire never sends personally identifiable information to any AI provider. Fields including owner names, occupant details, witness contact information, dates of birth, phone numbers, and addresses are stripped before any content is passed to an AI model.

The AI feature operates only on structural and investigative content — fire patterns, cause determinations, NFPA 921 references — never on the personal information of any party involved in a case.

You are responsible for reviewing the privacy policy of whichever AI provider you connect. DocuFire takes no responsibility for data handling by third-party AI services you choose to enable.

05

Subscriptions & Payments

DocuFire subscriptions are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We never receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card numbers, or Apple ID credentials. All billing is handled by Apple under their Privacy Policy.

06

Data Retention & Deletion

Because DocuFire stores all case data locally on your device, you are in complete control at all times. To delete your data, simply delete the app from your device. No account deletion request is necessary — no account is created and no case data is stored on our servers.

07

Children's Privacy

DocuFire is a professional tool intended for use by licensed fire investigators. It is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from minors.

08

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of DocuFire after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
