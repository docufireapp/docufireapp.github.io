EVERY FEATURE
YOU ACTUALLY NEED

Built by a working CFI. Not a software team guessing what investigators need.

⚡
Electrical Panel Documentation
Interactive breaker grid with up to 42 slots. Tap to cycle On/Off/Tripped/Unknown. Double-pole linking. Tripped breakers highlight red automatically.
🌤
Auto Weather Pull
Enter the loss address and date. DocuFire pulls historical weather conditions automatically — temperature, wind, precipitation, and more.
✍️
Apple Pencil Signatures
Joint scene exam sign-in sheets with live Apple Pencil signature capture. Business card camera built in. Exports as a formatted PDF sign-in sheet.
🔗
Chain of Custody
Full evidence log with container type, lab submission tracking, transfer history, storage conditions, and signature blocks for released/received by.
🤖
AI Analysis
Run an AI analysis on your completed case. Get cause determination review, inconsistency flags, suggested interview questions, and NFPA 921 references.
📄
Selective PDF Export
Choose exactly which sections go into each PDF. Send just the sign-in sheet to the adjuster. Send the full report to the attorney. One tap.
"BUILT BY AN INVESTIGATOR WHO GOT TIRED OF CLIPBOARDS — AND MAKING SURE NOTHING GOT MISSED."

DocuFire started as a frustration. There are no dedicated native iPad apps for fire investigation documentation. The one or two tools that exist are web-based — not built for the field, not built for gloves, not built for how investigations actually happen.

DocuFire is built by a CFI, for fire investigators at every level — CFIs, CFEIs, fire marshals, and anyone who documents origin and cause for a living. Every field, every workflow, and every tap target is designed around real investigative work.

Vehicle and boat investigation documentation coming soon.
